Shape Keyword Description
Circle Center Point in the middle of the circle
Diameter Line passing through the center of the circle and touches two points at the circumference
Radius Line from center to the circumference of the circle
Tangent Line that touches the circumference of the circle at a point
Chord Line that joins any two points on the circumference of the circle
Segment Region bounded by a chord and an arc
Arc Part of a circumference
Triangle Isosceles Triangle Triangle with two equal sides
Scalene Triangle All the sides of this triangle are not equal
Equilateral Triangle All sides of this triangle are equal
Right Angle Triangle Triangle with 90 degree angle
Interior angles Sum of interior angles of triangle add up to 180
Exterior angle Exterior angle of triangle equal to the sum of the opposite two interior angles
Congruent Triangles Triangles with same shape and size, same angles and same side lengths
Similar Triangles Triangles with same shapes but not same size, same angles but different size lengths.
Perpendicular Lines Two lines that meet or intersect at 90 degree angle They are represented by the sign ⊥
Parallel Lines Two lines pointing in the same direction that do not meet at any point They are sometimes intersected by traversal lines to form angles
They are represented by the sign ||
Traversal Lines Lines that intersect two or more lines at different points The form some angles
Corresponding Angles Angles that form F shape in the presence of traversal lines are equal
Alternate Angles Angles that form Z or N shape in the presence of traversal lines are equal
Co-interior Angles Angles that form U or C shape in the presence of traversal lines are equal
